Viewing File Transfer History

 

Introduction

In addition to enabling the managing of existing files, the XFM also enables you to see your file movement history. When a file transfer begins, it is logged and recorded by the XFM middleware and displayed for you in the History table at the bottom of the XFM. As the file transfer progresses, the progress bar of that transfer in the history table also progresses. You have full control over which transfers you keep and which you delete. The right click action items below describe the functionality you have at your disposal from this table. We offer no suggested use of this feature other than as a way to track where you have copied your files.  You may toggle the visibility of this panel by selecting View->Show/Hide Transfers from the top application menu.

 

Transfer Selected    

Repeat the listed transfer. If the credential is no longer valid or the file/destination no longer exists, this transfer will fail.

 

Refreshing Transfer History

Refresh your file transfer history.

               

Stop Transfer

Stop the transfer from continuing. If the transfer is already done, this method does nothing.

 

Remove Transfers

Remove the selected records from the history table and delete the records from the middleware.

 

Offline Mode

When working in Offline mode, the above descriptions hold. The only difference between the two scenarios is that when you work offline, your transfers are not saved for later use.
